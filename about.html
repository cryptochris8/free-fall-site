About Free Fall

Welcome to Free Fall, an exciting educational journey through the skies! In this thrilling math adventure, you'll dive through a vibrant tunnel filled with colorful voxel cubes, each displaying numbers to test your arithmetic skills. As you begin your descent, look to the top of the screen to see math questions appear. Quickly identify the correct answer among the four numbered cubes below and aim your fall toward it!
