About Free Fall

Where Education Meets Adventure

Welcome to Free Fall, an exciting educational journey through the skies! In this thrilling math adventure, you'll dive through a vibrant tunnel filled with colorful voxel cubes, each displaying numbers to test your arithmetic skills.

The Game Experience

As you begin your descent, look to the top of the screen to see math questions appear. Quickly identify the correct answer among the four numbered cubes below and aim your fall toward it!

You'll face a total of 10 arithmetic challenges, including addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division. Keep sharp and stay focused to ensure you select the correct answer each time.

Your reward? Safely landing at the surface after successfully completing all 10 questions!

Educational Impact
